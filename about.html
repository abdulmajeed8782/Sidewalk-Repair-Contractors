Our Story

Eden Contracting NY was founded with a simple mission: to provide New York City property owners with reliable, professional, and compliant sidewalk repair services. For over 15 years, we have been serving all five boroughs of NYC, helping thousands of property owners resolve DOT violations and maintain safe, beautiful sidewalks.

What started as a small family business has grown into one of NYC's most trusted sidewalk repair companies. Our success is built on a foundation of quality workmanship, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer service. We understand the unique challenges that NYC property owners face when dealing with sidewalk issues and DOT regulations, and we're here to make the process as smooth and stress-free as possible.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and compliant sidewalk repair services that protect our clients from liability while enhancing the safety and value of their properties. We are committed to excellence in every project, no matter how big or small.
